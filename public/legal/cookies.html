This Cookie Policy explains how Life Swap uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help websites function properly, analyze usage, and enhance security.

2. How We Use Cookies

Life Swap uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • **Authentication** – To keep you logged in.
  • **Analytics & Performance** – We use **Google Analytics** to track how users interact with the site.
  • **Security** – To detect and prevent fraudulent activity.
  • **Preferences** – To remember your settings and improve user experience.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow trusted third parties to place cookies on your device, including:

  • Google Analytics – For tracking website traffic.
  • Firebase – For authentication and database management.
  • PayPal – If you make a donation, PayPal’s cookies may apply.

These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies in your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as needed. Continued use of Life Swap after updates means you accept the changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about our cookie usage, visit our Contact Page.
